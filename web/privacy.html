QSpot Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 1, 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how QSpot Inc. collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use the QSpot app or website (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal information:

(a) Account Information — your name, email address, and profile photo from your Google or Apple account;
(b) Location Data — with your permission, to show you nearby spots and provide location-relevant features;
(c) Transaction Data — booking history, payment details (processed securely by our payment provider), and host earnings;
(d) Device Information — device type, operating system version, IP address, and app usage analytics;
(e) Communications — messages exchanged between Hosts and Renters on our platform;
(f) User Content — photos, listing descriptions, and reviews you submit.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

(a) Operate, maintain, and improve the Service;
(b) Process bookings, payments, and host payouts;
(c) Verify your identity and prevent fraud and abuse;
(d) Send transactional and service-related communications (booking confirmations, receipts, safety alerts);
(e) Personalize your experience and show relevant listings;
(f) Comply with applicable legal obligations;
(g) Send promotional communications where you have given consent or where permitted by law.

3. Information Sharing

We may share your personal information with:

(a) Other Users — Hosts see a Renter's first name and profile photo upon booking confirmation; Renters see Host-provided listing details and first name;
(b) Payment Processors — to facilitate secure transactions (e.g., Stripe);
(c) Service Providers — analytics, customer support, cloud infrastructure, and other vendors bound by confidentiality obligations;
(d) Legal Authorities — when required by applicable law, court order, or to protect QSpot's legal rights;
(e) Business Transfers — in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of all or a portion of QSpot's assets, with appropriate confidentiality protections.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. After account deletion, we may retain certain data for legal, tax, regulatory, or fraud-prevention purposes for up to seven (7) years, or longer where required by law.

5. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

(a) Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you;
(b) Correction — request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data;
(c) Deletion — request deletion of your data, subject to legal retention requirements;
(d) Portability — receive your data in a portable, machine-readable format;
(e) Opt-Out of Sale (California/CCPA) — we do not sell your personal information to third parties;
(f) Opt-Out of Sharing for Cross-Context Behavioral Advertising (CPRA);
(g) Canadian Residents (PIPEDA) — you may access and request correction of your personal information held by QSpot.

To exercise any of these rights, please email admin@qspotmarketplace.com. We will respond within the timeframes required by applicable law.

6. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to authenticate users, maintain sessions, analyze usage patterns, and improve the Service. You can manage cookie preferences through your device or browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of the Service.

7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information, including encryption of data in transit (TLS) and at rest, strict access controls, and regular security audits. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information. Please notify us immediately at admin@qspotmarketplace.com if you believe your account has been compromised.

8. Third-Party Links & Services

The Service may contain links to third-party websites or integrate with third-party services (e.g., Google Maps). This Privacy Policy does not apply to those third parties. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you access through QSpot.

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe that a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us at admin@qspotmarketplace.com and we will promptly delete such information.

10. International Data Transfers

QSpot operates in the United States and Canada. If you are accessing the Service from outside these jurisdictions, your personal information may be transferred to and processed in these countries, which may have different data protection laws than your home country. By using the Service, you consent to such transfers.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. We will notify you of significant changes by email or in-app notice. Your continued use of the Service after such changes take effect constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

12. Contact Us
