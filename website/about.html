Our story

The fair way to trade your place in line

QSpot Marketplace turns time spent waiting into something you can trade — openly, safely, and one-for-one.

Our mission

QSpot Marketplace started with a simple observation: waiting in line has real value, but there's never been a fair way to trade it. So we built one.

On QSpot Marketplace, someone who's already in line can sell their spot to someone who'd rather not wait. The catch that makes it fair — and the whole point — is that it's strictly one-for-one. When a buyer takes a spot, the seller steps out. The line stays the exact same length, and nobody behind moves back a single place. It's a swap, not a cut.

It works anywhere people line up. The person who shows up early can earn from the time they're spending anyway; the person who'd rather not wait can pay for it — and no one loses their place. A line is a line: lift lines, product drops, restaurants, box offices, service counters, festivals, and anywhere else people wait.

What we value

  • ⚖️
    Fair by design
    Every trade is one-for-one. The line never grows and no one behind loses their place.
  • 🛡️
    Trust & safety
    Identity verification, ratings, and payments processed and held by Stripe until a GPS-confirmed handoff mean you can trade with strangers confidently.
  • 🏠
    Locals first
    The people who know the lines best should earn from them. QSpot Marketplace puts money in the pockets of people who are already there.
  • 🚀
    Simple & universal
    List a spot in a couple of taps. If people wait for it, QSpot Marketplace works for it.
