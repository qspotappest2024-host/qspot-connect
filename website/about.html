About QSpot

We're building a marketplace that brings people together through real-world spots and services — locally, transparently, and simply.

Our Mission

QSpot was born from a simple idea: connecting people with the spaces and services around them shouldn't be complicated. Whether you have a parking spot to share, a service to offer, or you're looking for something nearby — QSpot makes that connection effortless.

We believe in empowering individuals and small businesses to monetize their spaces and skills, while giving buyers a trusted way to discover local opportunities they'd never find otherwise.

What We Value

  • 🌎
    Local First
    Everything starts with your neighborhood. We prioritize proximity and real-world connections over algorithms.
  • 🛡
    Trust & Safety
    Progressive identity verification and secure payments mean you can transact with confidence.
  • ⚖
    Fairness
    Sellers choose their pricing model. We keep fees transparent and never lock you in.
  • 🚀
    Simplicity
    List a spot in minutes. Find one even faster. No clutter, no complexity.
