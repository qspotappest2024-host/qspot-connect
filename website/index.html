Payments & security

Every trade, protected

Money only changes hands when a swap is real. Card payments, holds, and payouts are processed by Stripe — so your details stay private and your money stays protected.

🔒

Card data you never share

Payments are processed by Stripe, a global, PCI-certified payments provider. QSpot never sees or stores your card number.

⏸️

Pre-authorized holds

Reserve a QSpot Secure spot and Stripe places a temporary hold — a pre-authorization — on your card. You're only charged when the handoff is confirmed, and if the trade is cancelled the hold is released.

🛡️

QSpot Secure spots

Choose a Secure spot and QSpot processes the whole payment through Stripe, holding it safely until you and the seller complete the swap in person.

💰

Protected payouts

Sellers are paid out through Stripe once the trade is done, with a short window for questions before the money is released.

🦺

Verified identities

Optional identity checks powered by Stripe help confirm you're dealing with a real, verified person.

📵

Self-managed listings

Rather settle in person? Post a self-managed spot for a small upfront listing fee and arrange payment directly with your buyer.

Powered by Stripe
